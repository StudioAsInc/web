What is Bohudur?

Bohudur is a free and innovative payment automation tool designed to streamline payment automation for businesses of all sizes. It offers integration with popular mobile banking platforms like bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and Upay.

Key Features:

  • Multi-platform Support: Seamless integration with various payment solutions.
  • Offline Capabilities: Handles offline SMS saving and notifications.
  • Real-time Notifications: Alerts for payment updates and offline device events.

Bohudur simplifies financial operations, making it a valuable tool for businesses seeking secure and efficient payment processing.
